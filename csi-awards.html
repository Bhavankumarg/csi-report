CSI Awards

The prestigious CSI Awards took centre stage on day two, celebrating extraordinary stories from community champions. The CSI Awards not only recognised past achievements but also ignited a renewed commitment to driving meaningful, positive change in communities across India.

The Best Social Impact Organisation Award

The People’s Action for National Integration (PANI) for their commendable work in empowering women, adolescent girls, farmers, and vulnerable communities at the grassroots level

The The Association for Advocacy and Legal Initiatives Trust (AALI) for their unwavering advocacy for women’s human rights and gender equality

The Best Social Impact Organisation Award

The Indian Grameen Services for 25 years of dedicated service

The Buzz Women received a special jury mention for their remarkable efforts in empowering 6,00,000 women over the years
