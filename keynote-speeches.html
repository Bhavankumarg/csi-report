The insights and experiences shared by our speakers in the morning’s plenaries were expressions that wove into the day’s proceedings.

Mr. Balasubramaniam

Member-HR, Capacity Building Commission, Govt of India (GoI), and Chairperson of Social Stock Exchange Advisory Committee, SEBI

Discover Mr. Balasubramaniam’s vision for collaborative innovation and sustainability in his stirring keynote at Catalysing Social Impact 2024 (Put an arrow)

He further noted that in India we tend to have an urban syndrome. He underscored the need to measure what we do to society since good intentions need not necessarily lead to good outcomes. Emphasising the word “catalyse”, he advised that we must not only catalyse the society we know but also listen to community voices to truly address their needs.

Aakash Patil

Director of the Dr. Ambedkar International Centre (DAIC)

Explore Director Aakash Patil’s insights on how the DAIC transcends conventional roles to drive socio-economic transformation and advance Buddhist studies.

Saritaji

A prominent leader from Jharkhand.

Learn about Saritaji’s transformative work with women in Jharkhand, empowering them to lead in gram sabhas and enhance their societal roles.

Mr. Aditya

Esteemed guest
