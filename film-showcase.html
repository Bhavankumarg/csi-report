Film Showcase

The public health communication showcase at CSI 2024 featured a diverse array of impactful films and discussions centred on key themes such as climate change, health, resilience, and community empowerment. Filmmakers and speakers shared insights on their creative process and ethical considerations, focusing on authenticity, respect, and community representation in storytelling.

Gutter Ki Macchli

The showcase included powerful films such as "Slowing the Spread of Dengue Fever" highlighting innovative disease prevention strategies, "Under the Indian Sky" shedding light on climate’s impact on frontline health workers, and "Gutter Ki Macchli" showcasing urban community experiences of climate change through animation.

Economic Resilience

Several films spotlighted the intersection of climate change and health, including "Impact of Climate Change on Sundarbans People" portraying the struggles of communities facing health challenges due to environmental shifts. "Economic Resilience", documented the resilience of fishing communities and the vital role of economic stability in livelihoods.

Dumpster Diving with Jaggery

A unique focus on women’s voices in the garment industry was presented in “Cotton’s Hidden Voices,” illustrating the undervalued contributions of women in textile supply chains. The showcase also featured engaging documentaries like “Dumpster Diving with Jaggery” and “Beyond the Clinic” emphasising sustainable practices and holistic well-being.

The films sparked deep reflections on gratitude, resilience, collaboration, and hope, showcasing the transformative power of storytelling in raising awareness and driving social change. The showcase served as a platform to amplify diverse voices, foster community connections, and inspire meaningful action towards a healthier and more sustainable future for all.

This immersive showcase not only highlighted pressing public health issues but also celebrated stories of resilience, innovation, and community empowerment, emphasising the profound impact of effective communication strategies in driving positive health outcomes and social transformation.
